Rules & Regulations

Please read the following guidelines carefully before proceeding with your orders or inquiries.

1 Order Confirmations

Once an order is placed via DMC Stores, RU Online, or Laaba Kade, it cannot be canceled after 24 hours. Please verify your items carefully.

2 Dropshipping Policy (RU Online)

For dropshipping items, delivery times may vary based on the supplier location. We are not responsible for delays caused by customs or international shipping carriers.

3 Electric Products (Laaba Kade)

All electric items come with a standard warranty. Do not attempt to repair electric devices yourself as this will void the warranty. Contact support for repairs.

4 Payments & Refunds

We only accept payments via verified methods (Bank Transfer/Cash on Delivery). Refunds are processed within 7-14 working days if the product is defective.

5 Respect & Conduct

Please respect our staff. Abusive language or behavior towards our 200+ team members will result in an immediate ban from our services.
